How We Can Help You

At BSR Studios, our priority is ensuring that our users have a seamless, highly stable experience while customizing their screens. Whether you are using our web directory to find the perfect background, or utilizing our desktop software, we are here to support you.

  • Wallpaper Requests: Can't find a specific animated background? Drop us a message with the game, anime, or nature scene you are looking for, and our team will work on adding a seamless loop of it to our library.
  • Software Support & Bug Reports: If you experience any issues downloading files, or if you encounter bugs while using AnyVidWall or AnyWebWall, please let us know. Include your basic system details to help us troubleshoot efficiently.
  • Business & Copyright Inquiries: For partnerships, content removals (DMCA), or collaboration requests, please ensure you leave a detailed message and a valid reply email.

Response Time: Our standard response time is between 24 to 48 hours. We read every single email we receive and appreciate your patience as we work to improve the AnyWallpaper experience.
